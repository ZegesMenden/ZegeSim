NOTES FOR SITL WITH CALLISTO

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

standard message structure

# as first character specifies that the message being sent is 'data' and not a message to the terminal or to callisto

all messages sent by callisto that do not begin with a # are forwarded to the terminal

all messages use SI units (radians, meters, meters per second, etc..)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

the standard message structure for messages TO callisto FROM the SITL computer are:

#msgid,pos_x,pos_y,pos_z,ori_x,ori_y,ori_z,ori_rate_x,ori_rate_y,ori_rate_z,accel_L_x,accel_L_y,accel_L_z,baro_alt,heading,GPS_y,GPS_z

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

the standard message structure for messages TO the SITL computer FROM callisto are:

#msgid,pyro_1,pyro_2,pyro_3,servo_out_1,servo_out_2,servo_out_3,servo_out_4

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
